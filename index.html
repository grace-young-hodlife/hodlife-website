Notice of Sandbox Status

HODLife Insurance Company Ltd (“HODLife”) is currently operating within the Bermuda Monetary Authority's Regulatory Sandbox with a Class ILT Insurer licence. The Sandbox is an innovation track for companies looking to test new technologies or business models on a limited number of clients in a controlled environment for a limited period of time. The BMA applies the same rigorous, risk-based supervisory approach to all insurers. However, because the company is still building its operations and testing its innovative business model, during this period policyholders may face higher risks than with a fully licensed, more established insurer. There is a risk that HODLife does not graduate from the Sandbox, in which case its operations will cease and all collected premiums from policies will need to be refunded. HODLife has set aside a reserve with the intention of returning the collected bitcoin premiums in that event; however, unexpected events could still occur that risk loss to Policyholders. The detailed rules of what will happen, in the event that HODLife does not graduate from the Sandbox, are set out in an Addendum to each policy issued during the period that HODLife remains in the Sandbox.
